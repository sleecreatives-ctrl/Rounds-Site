Dump your notes
Paste whatever you've jotted — messy, abbreviated, out of order. Rounds structures it into a complete handoff note per patient. Copy to your EHR. Go home.
~5 minutes for 6 patients
Morning brief generator
Paste your patient assignment. Get a one-paragraph clinical summary per patient — organized by acuity, ready in seconds.
AI clinical suggestions
Rounds reads what you've documented and surfaces specific flags — post-op day 3 with no BM, BP trending up, discharge tomorrow without PT/OT. The things that fall through the cracks.
SBAR generator
Complete Situation, Background, Assessment, Recommendation — generated from what's already in the system. Read it, adapt it, make the call.
Care team thread
A per-patient thread for nurses, attendings, and charge nurses. Replaces phone tag and lost pages. Everyone sees the same real-time picture.
Shift handoff generator
Dump your end-of-shift notes in any format. Rounds structures them into a complete, professional handoff per patient. Copy to your EHR and go home on time.
